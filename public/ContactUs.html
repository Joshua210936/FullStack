Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the buying process?

You can start by browsing our extensive listings to discover properties that match your preferences and needs.

While optional, we recommend partnering with a property agent on our website to facilitate a smoother process. They can offer valuable insights and guidance every step of the way.

Once you’ve found a property you’re interested in, either you or your agent can reach out to the seller to negotiate the price and arrange viewings.

If you’re satisfied with the property and terms, proceed with the purchase to make the property yours.

What are the additional costs involved in purchasing a property?

When purchasing a property through our marketplace, buyers can rejoice in the fact that there are no additional fees charged by us. The seller is responsible for a 2% fee on the base price of the property, ensuring a transparent and buyer-friendly transaction. Please note that while our platform does not impose any extra charges on buyers, standard property purchasing costs such as stamp duty, legal fees, and mortgage-related expenses still apply and are handled by the seller.

How can I list a property on Sigma Properties?

First, create a Sigma Properties account as a seller. Then click on list properties in the navbar and fill in the details. After submitting, give 3 working days for our staff to check the listing and approve it.
